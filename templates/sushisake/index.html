ABOUT US

Sushi & Sake draws its inspiration directly from the vibrant streets of Japan. We pride ourselves by pairing the tantalising tastes of the Japanese cuisine with a carefully cultured environment to provide a true culinary experience.

The sushi...

Take a seat in our enchanting restaurant and let our expert chefs prepare you a variety of Japanese delights. Indulge yourself in our delectable handmade sushi and sashimi, colourful poke bowls, golden katsu, bouncy bao buns and much more...

Sushi & Sake also prides itself on providing a range of mouth-watering vegetarian and vegan options for those wanting to experience the fantasy without the fish!

...And the sake

Not only does our restaurant provide a sumptious offering of Japanese cuisine, but as the name might suggest, we also have a superb selection drinks such as sake! Our day-to-night restaurant provides a magical ambience for those seeking an indulgent evening with an oritental twist. We offer a range of tipples from sakes, plum-wines and Japanese-inspired cocktails.
